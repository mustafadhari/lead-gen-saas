## Service Tiers

### 🥉 Starter — Landing Page
- **What**: Single-page site with hero, services, reviews, contact form
- **Turnaround**: 24–48 hours
- **Price range**: $300 – $600
- **Best for**: Local businesses, solo practitioners, new businesses

### 🥈 Growth — Multi-Page Website
- **What**: 3–6 page site (Home, About, Services, Contact, Blog)
- **Turnaround**: 5–7 business days
- **Price range**: $800 – $2,000
- **Best for**: Established small businesses wanting a professional presence

### 🥇 Pro — Custom Web Application
- **What**: Full custom build — e-commerce, booking systems, dashboards
- **Turnaround**: 2–4 weeks
- **Price range**: $2,500 – $8,000+
- **Best for**: Businesses needing custom functionality
