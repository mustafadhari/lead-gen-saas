# Objection Handling — Quick Reference

## Phone Objections

### "I'm not interested"
> "Totally fine — can I send the mockup over email so you have it? No strings attached."
- **Goal**: Convert to email lead
- **Outcome code**: `not_interested` (or `maybe` if they accept the email)

### "I already have a website"
> "That's great! I took a look at it actually — I had a couple of ideas that could help with conversions. Can I show you on a quick 5-minute video call tomorrow?"
- **Goal**: Reframe as improvement, not replacement. Schedule a follow-up demo call.
- **Outcome code**: `interested` or `not_interested`

### "How much does it cost?"
> "Depends on the scope, but landing pages start at $300 and full sites at $800. The mockup I made is free either way — want me to send it over?"
- **Goal**: Anchor low, redirect to the free mockup
- **Outcome code**: `interested` or `maybe`

### "Just send me an email"
> "Absolutely, I'll send it right over with the live preview link. What email works best for you?"
- **Goal**: Capture their preferred email, move to email sequence
- **Outcome code**: `maybe`

### "I don't have time right now"
> "No worries at all — how about a quick 5-minute video call tomorrow? I'll share my screen and walk you through the mockup. What time works best for you?"
- **Goal**: Schedule a follow-up demo call for the next day
- **Outcome code**: `callback` or `interested`

### "How did you get my number?"
> "Your business is listed on [Google Maps / Yelp / your website]. I saw it, thought I could help, and put together a free mockup. Happy to take you off my list if you'd prefer."
- **Goal**: Be transparent, offer removal
- **Outcome code**: `not_interested` or `do_not_contact`

### "I've been burned by web developers before"
> "I completely understand — that's exactly why I build the mockup first. You see the work before spending a cent. No contracts, no lock-in."
- **Goal**: Address trust with proof of work
- **Outcome code**: `interested` or `maybe`

### "Can you do it cheaper?"
> "I keep my prices fair because I do quality work fast. That said, we can always start with just a landing page and expand later — that's my most popular option."
- **Goal**: Downsell to starter tier, don't discount
- **Outcome code**: `interested`

---

## Email Objections

### No reply after 3 emails
- Move to `re-engagement` sequence (30-day cooldown)
- Do **not** keep emailing — it damages deliverability

### "Remove me from your list"
- Immediately set status to `do_not_contact`
- Reply: "Done — you've been removed. Sorry for the interruption."
- **Never** email them again

### "I'm happy with my current provider"
- Reply: "Great to hear! The mockup is yours to keep if you ever want a second opinion. Best of luck!"
- Mark as `not_interested`

---

## Rules

1. **Never argue** — if they say no twice, it's a no
2. **Never discount unprompted** — only downsell to a smaller tier
3. **Always offer the free mockup** — it's the strongest objection breaker
4. **Log every objection** — patterns reveal what to fix in the pitch
5. **Respect "do not contact"** — immediately and permanently
